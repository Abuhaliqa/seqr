Welcome to matchbox! The bridge to the Matchmaker Exchange at the Center for Mendelian Genomics at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard (Broad CMG). To use matchbox, you must be a collaborator and have your data deposited in the seqr platform

Our software is freely available, open source and can be found here. We encourage all new centers interested in joining the Matchmaker Exchange to please use this software and contribute their effort towards making this a community resource.

matchbox is currently maintained by the Broad CMG, and the Monarch Initiative.

Please contact us at matchmaker@broad.
